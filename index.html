About

iGEM

iGEM, standing for International Genetically Engineered Machine, is the largest synthetic biology competition in the world where multidisciplinary teams are working on designing techniques and building biological devices that will bring novel solutions to everyday issues facing the world. By working on the projects, teams are contributing to an open registry of standard biological parts and the whole competition is stimulating synthetic biology research and entrepreneurship in biotechnology. Teams are also encouraged to collaborate with leaders in the industry and with each other, for the exchange of knowledge and skills. By virtue of marketing and human practices, students are motivated to bring synthetic biology outside the lab by interacting with stakeholders, local communities, schools and many more. iGEM Stockholm

The iGEM Stockholm team is driven entirely by the team as an extracurricular project and participating students can come from any university in Stockholm.

Roles

For good project management, the team is usually divided into subteams which are responsible for different parts of the project. Sometimes people are in only one subteam while some can be in multiple subteams, but these roles give a good picture of what kind of responsibilities a typical Stockholm team has:

Leader

There are 1-2 leaders who are responsible for communication with the universities and SGEM, managing the team and recruiting the members. But of course they also play a crucial role in any subteam they would like to be part of.

Wetlab

The members of the wet-lab subteam are responsible for preparing protocols, performing experiments and finding supporting literature.

Drylab

Everything digital is handled by our dry-lab subteam. They are in charge of the wiki, models, designs and in situ experiments - to list a few tasks!

Human Practices

HP is the link between the team and the broader community, gathering feedback and opinions for the project idea and continually making sure that the project responds to actual needs from stakeholders.

Media

Communication is key, so the media team takes charge of all graphic design, social media and video work in order to present the team’s work with an artistic touch.

Finance

The people in charge of keeping the crazy minds of the researchers in check are the finance members. They are in charge of the finances and fundings.
